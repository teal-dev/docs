The [Developer Portal](https://developer.teal.dev/) is where you can configure settings for your accounting platform.

- [Chart of accounts](/configuration/chart_of_accounts_config)
- [Auto-categorization settings](/configuration/categorization_config)
- [External data sources](/configuration/data_sources_config)
- [Create a test user](/configuration/user_config) with programmatically generated sample data for testing purposes

Once you have configured your accounting platform, you can use the [Create User endpoint](/api-reference/users/create_user) to [create and configure a new user](/configuration/user_config).
